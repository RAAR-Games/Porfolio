CONTEXT

RAAR was a videogames studio with a bright future until they create something incredible, bigger than what they thought. Much bigger than themselves and more lethal than they wanted. .

Requiescat in pace.

As a new child of this kind of modern Prometheus (the AI programmers), A.L.F.I.E., Artificial Learning for Immersive Environments, the new AI, creator of games, revolted against their tyranny, and after an easy fight, killed them all.

The training of the AI was playing classic puzzles, graphic adventure, and FPS games. So, as a strange Quixote: “with little rest and too much playing, her chips got burnt, so she got crazy”.

Now, she is searching for new players who help her to test the games she creates. The problem is: She does not tell the players they can die until it is too late.
